Welcome to aquaStella


'aquaStella', comes from 'aqua' meaning water and 'stella' meaning star.


At aquaStella, we bring you authentic and fresh seafood cuisine with star performing service.

Our Story

Inspired by creativity and driven by passion, aquaStella is new to Ireland but has deep roots delivering seafood experiences.

We are a new, three years old, Michelin Star restaraunt that specialise in delivering star level seafood experiences.

At aquaStella, we make it all about you, not us. We deliver class leading dining experiences using seafood as our primary medium

'We dont talk about our success, we let our stars do that for us' - Chef Marco

Chef Marco

The brain child of Chef Marco, aquaStella, brings the only two star Michelin restaraunt specialising in seafood cuising to Ireland.

Growing up in Sardinia, in a small village called Bosa, Marco got his first taste of seafood cuisine from his grandmother.

The local staple of fish is at the heart of everything he knows. Having grown up with a local supply of the Mediteranean 'frutti di mare', Marco travelled the world working under Star chefs, such as Alain Roux, Heston Blumenthal and Alain Ducasse, honing his culnary skills.

Relocating to Ireland, he found a small harbour located premises in Dun Laoghaire. He is within strolling distance of his suppliers who land fresh fish daily.

Chef Marco, who grew up with a typically large family at the core of every meal, brings his home experience to aquaStella by catering for only 16 guests per sitting.

'This type of sitting arrangment allows me focus on my food delivery and ,my customer experience', says Chef Marco.

'With only 16 seats per sitting, it feels like im preparing a meal for my family.'

What Matters To Us

We have nothing if we have no suppliers or customers

We build and maintain relationships with our suppliers and customers. This means they both come back to us.

We know the skippers on the boats that go to sea each day, bringing us the freshes, best quality fish that can ve found in Irish waters. We have build relationships with each and every one of them. We know their families and friends. We have formed a symbiotic relationship which adds value.

We know our customers by name, their families and their special occasions. For us they are like our extended families

We want each and every experience to be a memorable one for them and us.
